MOTIVATION

I’m motivated by the journey from “could this work?” to “this is ready to build.” I enjoy shaping concepts into practical designs thinking through fit, alignment, and manufacturability so the first build isn’t a surprise and the tenth build is effortless.

What keeps me excited is the loop of build-learn-refine. I like chasing root causes, validating choices with analysis and testing, and iterating until the design feels “quiet” in production reliable, repeatable, and ready to ship.
